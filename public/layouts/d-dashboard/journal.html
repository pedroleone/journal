Today felt like a turning point. The conversation with Maria about the project direction was exactly what I needed to hear. She has this way of cutting through the noise and getting to what actually matters.

I keep thinking about what she said about intention — that most of what we call planning is just anxiety wearing a calendar. There's something almost embarrassing about how much that landed for me, like I'd been waiting to hear it said out loud for years.

We talked for almost two hours. The coffee went cold. The café filled up around us and neither of us noticed until someone asked if they could take the chairs at the next table.

I'm going to try to hold onto this feeling — not the conclusions, but the quality of the thinking. How it felt to work through something hard without knowing the answer in advance. That's rare. I want to be better at making room for it.

Tomorrow I need to follow up on the API work, but tonight I just want to sit with this.
