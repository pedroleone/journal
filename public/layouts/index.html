Journal App — UI/UX Explorations

5 radically different layout concepts for a private single-user journal app

C

The Workspace

Multi-pane IDE-style layout. Open Journal and Food side-by-side. Icon rail navigation. Each domain as a resizable panel.

multi-pane icon rail cross-domain context
D

The Daily Dashboard

Day-centric 2×2 hub. Today is always home. Each quadrant previews a domain — click to expand into a full view.

day-centric 2×2 grid daily snapshot
F

The Rooms

Each domain is a fully self-contained room with its own layout, color, and personality. The journal room feels like a writing app. The food room feels like a meal tracker.

unique per domain full immersion custom layouts
G

The Notebook

Physical notebook metaphor with tabbed section dividers. Warm paper textures, lined writing surface, index-card library. Your personal book.

analog feel warm palette tactile
J

The Dock

Content fills the screen. A persistent status bar at the bottom shows live metrics for every domain — you always know what's happening without leaving your current view.

ambient awareness maximized content live status dock
